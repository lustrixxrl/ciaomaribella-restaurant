# PRD: Ciao Maribella Restaurant Website

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

- **Project name:** Ciao Maribella Ristorante
- **One line description:** Elegant Northern Italian restaurant website showcasing authentic Florentine cuisine and warm hospitality in Sioux Falls, SD
- **Primary goal:** Drive reservations and communicate the restaurant's upscale Italian dining experience to potential guests

## TARGET USER

- **Who is the main user:** Adults 25-65, middle to upper income, food enthusiasts seeking authentic Italian dining experiences for date nights, celebrations, or business dinners
- **What problem are they coming to solve:** Find a high-quality Italian restaurant, view menu and pricing, check hours/location, and make a reservation
- **What does success look like for them:** They book a reservation, find the menu and hours easily, and arrive at the restaurant excited for the experience

## PAGES AND STRUCTURE

### Page name: Home/Landing Page
- **Purpose:** Create immediate emotional connection, showcase ambiance, drive reservations
- **Key elements:** 
  - Hero section with stunning food/interior imagery
  - Tagline: "Entri come amici, Vada come famiglia" (Enter as friends, leave as family)
  - Quick reservation CTA
  - Awards badges (OpenTable Diners' Choice, Wine Spectator)
  - Brief about section
  - Preview of menu highlights
  - Footer with contact, hours, location

### Page name: Menu
- **Purpose:** Display full menu with categories and pricing
- **Key elements:**
  - All 8 categories: Antipasti, Insalate, Pizze, Primi, Secondi, Contorni, Dolci, Menu Degustazione
  - Item descriptions and prices
  - Dietary indicators (vegetarian, gluten-free if applicable)
  - Wine pairing suggestions

### Page name: About
- **Purpose:** Tell the story of owners Riccardo and Marybeth, establish authenticity
- **Key elements:**
  - Riccardo's Florentine origins
  - Journey from Vespa Catering to Maribella (2022)
  - Philosophy of hospitality
  - Photos of owners/interior
  - Awards and recognition

### Page name: Contact & Reservations
- **Purpose:** Enable bookings and provide location info
- **Key elements:**
  - Reservation widget/form
  - Address: 360 S. Main Ave. Sioux Falls, SD 57104
  - Phone: (605)271-1710
  - Google Maps embed
  - Full hours grid
  - Parking info

## FEATURES — MUST HAVE

1. **Reservation system** — Allow users to book tables directly or link to reservation platform
2. **Full menu display** — Browseable menu with all categories and items
3. **Hours and location** — Clear display of operating hours and address
4. **Mobile responsive design** — Flawless experience on all devices
5. **Click-to-call phone** — Mobile users can tap to call
6. **Google Maps integration** — Embedded map for directions

## FEATURES — NICE TO HAVE

1. **Online ordering integration** — For takeout orders
2. **Gift card purchase** — E-commerce for gift cards
3. **Wine Club signup** — Email capture for wine club
4. **Private events inquiry form** — For catering and large parties
5. **Instagram feed** — Live social media integration
6. **Newsletter signup** — Email marketing list

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

- **Overall feel:** Elegant, warm, inviting, authentic Italian sophistication
- **Color preference:** Deep burgundy/wine red, cream, gold accents, dark charcoal — inspired by Italian wine and Tuscan sunsets
- **Font style:** Elegant serif for headings (Playfair Display or similar), clean sans-serif for body (Lato, Source Sans)
- **Reference sites I like:** 
  - https://www.ciaomaribella.com/ (current site)
  - High-end Italian restaurant sites with photography focus
- **What to avoid:** Cluttered layouts, cheap-looking templates, overly casual aesthetics, generic stock photos

## TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

- **Authentication needed:** No
- **Database needed:** No (static content site)
- **Payments needed:** No (unless gift cards added later)
- **Mobile responsive:** Yes — always
- **Any integrations:** 
  - Google Maps
  - Reservation platform (OpenTable/Resy or custom form)
  - Google Analytics
  - Social media links

## CONTENT

- **Will you provide copy or should AI generate it:** Use existing content from current website, AI to enhance/polish
- **Images:** Real images from restaurant (food plating, interior, staff, owners)
- **Logo:** Use existing Ciao Maribella logo from current site

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

1. A first-time visitor understands this is an upscale Italian restaurant within 5 seconds
2. Reservation CTA is visible and functional on all pages
3. Menu loads quickly and is readable on mobile
4. Hours and location are findable in under 10 seconds
5. Site loads in under 3 seconds on mobile
6. All images are optimized and display correctly
7. Contact phone is clickable on mobile
